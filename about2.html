From Legal Insight to Integrated Solution

Founded in 2015 by a small team of legal innovators and developers, our company was born from a simple frustration: why were attorneys forced to choose between complex, siloed systems and outdated manual processes? We set out to create intuitive, unified technology—starting with our flagship CLIO (Consultation) and MYCASE (Case Management) products.

Through years of rapid growth, key product expansions (including the powerful Analytics suite), and continuous collaboration with the legal community, we have grown into a trusted global partner. Our journey is defined by relentless innovation and an unwavering commitment to making complex legal challenges manageable and scalable.

Today, we proudly empower thousands of legal professionals worldwide, but our startup ethos remains: agility, curiosity, and a deep dedication to customer success and simplifying the practice of law.

Our Mission

To empower law firms and legal professionals of all sizes with integrated, intelligent software solutions that simplify complex legal operations and drive sustainable growth and client success.

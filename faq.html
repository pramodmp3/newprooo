What is the core difference between CLIO and MYCASE, and do they work together?

▼

CLIO is your platform for expert legal consultation and strategic service delivery. MYCASE is the portal that gives clients and attorneys a secure, real-time view of all case files, documents, and communication. They are fully integrated to manage the entire legal process.

How does ANALYTICS enhance the performance of both CLIO and MYCASE?

▼

ANALYTICS acts as the firm's business intelligence hub. It uses data from both platforms to provide real-time insights into firm profitability, resource allocation, and case efficiency, enabling proactive, data-driven decisions.

Is the STACKLY platform secure for handling sensitive client and case data?

▼

Yes, we prioritize security with bank-grade encryption and multi-factor authentication. The cloud-based system ensures all confidential legal and financial data is protected and compliant with industry standards.

Can MYCASE be customized to fit the unique workflow of our specific law practice area?

▼

Yes, it is highly customizable. You can tailor document templates, automate workflows, and adjust case status fields to perfectly match your firm’s specific practice area and procedures.

What is the typical onboarding and data migration process when adopting the STACKLY suite?

Our dedicated team handles the entire process, including secure data migration from your existing systems. We then provide comprehensive training for all users to ensure rapid adoption and full optimization of the integrated platform
